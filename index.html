Who we are

A nonprofit built for tomorrow's leaders

Youth Empower Program (YEP) is a nonprofit youth organisation of Bangladesh. Our Virtual Internship programs and completely free training sessions are open to university students from every university across Bangladesh, helping them become genuinely job-ready wherever they study.

Inside our internships, participants work on academic research, market research, startup research, and real IT project building — while developing the human side of professional life: team management, critical thinking, time management, presentation skills, public speaking, and emotional intelligence.

Our mission — to develop leadership among young people, nurture entrepreneurship, and push the next generation forward in research and innovation.

Our structure

Three departments, one mission

Every intern and volunteer works within one of our three core departments, each building a different set of real-world capabilities.

Research & Innovation

Academic, market and startup research projects that sharpen analytical thinking and evidence-based decision making.

IT Department

Hands-on IT project building — from small tools to full applications — under the guidance of experienced mentors.

Content Writing & Creative

Storytelling, design and communication — the creative engine that carries YEP's message to a wider audience.
